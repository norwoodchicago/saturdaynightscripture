Blessed are you when people hate you, and when they exclude and insult you, and denounce your name as evil.

Matthew 5:13

Rejoice and be glad, for your reward will be great in heaven.

You are the salt of the earth. But if salt loses its taste, with what can it be seasoned? It is no longer good for anything but to be thrown out and trampled underfoot.

You are the light of the world. A city set on mountain cannot be hidden. Nor do they light lamp and then put it under bushel basket. It is set on lampstand, where it gives light to all in the house.

Just so, your light must shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your heavenly Father.

(Matt. 13) He spoke to them another parable. "The kingdom of heaven is like yeast that woman took and mixed with three measures of wheat flour until the whole batch was leavened."
