First remove the beam out of your own eye.

God, I need your help. Please help me now.

Glory be to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, as it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be. Amen. Alleluiah.

Psalm 47

Clap your hands, all you nations. Shout to God with the voice of triumph! For Yahweh Most High is awesome. He is a great King over all the earth. He subdues nations under us, and peoples under our feet. He chooses our inheritance for us, the glory of Jacob whom he loved.

God has gone up with a shout, Yahweh with the sound of a trumpet. Sing praises to God! Sing praises! Sing praises to our King! Sing praises! For God is the King of all the earth. Sing praises with understanding. God reigns over the nations. God sits on his holy throne. The princes of the peoples are gathered together, the people of the God of Abraham. For the shields of the earth belong to God. He is greatly exalted!

Let us pray

Father in Heaven, it is you who are my God. I put all things in your hands today. Be with me this day as my guide, my light, my strength, my defender. Amen.

Matthew 7:2

"The judgement you apply to others will be applied to you."

"Don't judge, so that you won't be judged. For with whatever judgment you judge, you will be judged; and with whatever measure you measure, it will be measured to you.

Why do you see the splinter that is in your brother's eye, but don't consider the two-by-four that is in your own eye? Or how will you tell your brother, 'Let me remove the speck from your eye,' and behold, the plank is in your own eye? You hypocrite!

First remove the beam out of your own eye, and then you can see clearly to remove the speck out of your brother's eye."

Hear our prayers

Father in heaven, please help me today in all things that I do. I place all my challenges and problems in your hands. I put my trust in you. Lord, hear my prayer.

Father in heaven, please help men everywhere with their problems today, especially those facing the worst situations and hardest challenges. Be with them. Lord, hear my prayer.

Jesus, show me my faults and help me fix them. Lord, hear my prayer.

Jesus, I trust in you.

Our Father in Heaven, hallowed be your name. Your kingdom come, your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread, and forgive us our sins, as we forgive those who wrong us. Protect us from temptation and deliver us from evil.
