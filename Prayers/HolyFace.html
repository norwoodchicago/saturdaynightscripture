In the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. Amen.

You are hunched over, in pain, paraded in front of me. Over your head, I see the angry sneers on their faces as they shout insults and throw things at you. At a different time in my life, I was in that crowd. I am truly sorry.

When you look down it seems like the accusations are directed at me. Your face is not protected. Am I accused by the crowd too? You are silent. I will be silent.

A woman comes to you with a veil and wipes your face. She is not angry. She is sad. She looks at you with love and wipes your face. But your face is beaten up and ugly. What does she see?

I hand you your cross back. You are blamed, you are wrong, it's your fault. Let them blame me. Let them prove that I'm wrong. Let them be better than me. Each acccuation directed at me makes me more like you. I want to be like you. I want to be on your side.

Most of all I want each person in this crowd to set aside their anger, their need to be right, and their need to win an argument. I will go first.

Jesus told Gertrude, If anyone meditates upon my sufferings with tenderness and compassion, his heart will be to me as a soothing balm for these wounds. Saint Gertrude, pray for us.

Jesus told Mechtilde, that if one ponders the holy face, the splendor of my countenance will be their eternal rejoicing. Saint Mechtilde, pray for us.

May the most holy and sacred name of God be in first place in my heart to the exclusion of everything else. When I look in my heart for this name, may I see the face of Jesus.

You saw the face of Jesus in the poor, and you tried to be the face of Jesus to those who didn't know him. Saint Teresa of Kolkata, help us in this.

Who will help us worship Jesus in the Eucharist? Teres of the Child Jesus and of the Holy Face, please pray for us.

The mother of Jesus was shocked, horrified and grieved to see her son beaten up. Wounded heart of Mary, pray for us.

Thief on the cross who begged for mercy, you saw the face of Jesus and converted on the spot. Pray for us.

Father in heaven I offer you my tiny sacrifice, my insignificant sacrifice. I do it to honor the face of Jesus that took so many punches and insults, and I offer you his sacrifice, for all the angry, wounded people who don't know your love.

Jesus I trust in you. Jesus I trust in your mercy.
