The fifteen stations

  1. Jesus is condemned to death
  2. Jesus picks up his cross
  3. Jesus falls
  4. Jesus meets his mother
  5. Simon of Cyrene helps carry the cross
  6. Veronica wipes the face of Jesus
  7. Jesus falls a second time
  8. Jesus meets the women of Jerusalem
  9. Jesus falls a third time
  10. Jesus is stripped
  11. Jesus is nailed to the cross
  12. Jesus hangs on the cross in darkness [to make 15]
  13. Jesus dies on the cross
  14. The body of Jesus is taken off the cross and handed to his mother
  15. Jesus is laid in the tomb
