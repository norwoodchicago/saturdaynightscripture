Saint Joseph, you are the faithful protector and intercessor of all who love and venerate you. You know that I have confidence in you. After Jesus and Mary, I look to you as an example for holiness, for you are especially close to God. I humbly commend myself, my family and all that belongs to me to your intercession. I ask you, by your love for Jesus and Mary, to stay close to me and assist in this time of need.

Glorious Saint Joseph, spouse of Mary, pray for me to have a chaste, pure, humble, charitable, alert mind, and perfect resignation to the divine will. Be my coach through life that I may not disappoint Jesus and Mary.

Saint Joseph, I ask your intercession in obtaining from the Divine Heart of Jesus the graces necessary for my spiritual and temporal welfare, especially the grace to give mercy and forgiveness to others, and the special request __________.

Guardian of the Word Incarnate, I feel confident that your prayers on my behalf will be heard before the throne of God.

St. Joseph, you tried the inn, and ended up with a barn. St. Jospeh of great faith, pray for us.

St. Joseph, you showed up in Egypt hungry, tired, and broke. St. Joseph who doesn't give up, pray for us.

St. Joseph, your life didn't turn out the way you expected. St. Joseph who puts himself a distant third, pray for us.

St. Joseph, you couldn't afford a lamb, so you brought 2 turtledoves. St. Joseph humble and poor, pray for us.

St. Joseph, your actions spoke louder than words. St. Joseph, holy and geniune, pray for us.

St. Joseph, God gave you a job to do and the gifts to do it. St. Joseph, dedicated and trustworthy, pray for us.

St. Joseph, you wanted to be there for Jesus until the end, but you weren't allowed. St. Joseph obedient with your last breath, pray for us.
